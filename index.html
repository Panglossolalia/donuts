Doughnuts/Donuts

Concept

  1. Arrive at doughnut place.
  2. Eat doughnut (or a half-doughnut -- it's in the title after all).
  3. Run to another doughnut place
  4. Go to step 1.
Assuming 225 calories per half-doughnut, this event should be approximately energy-neutral after considering the 600 meters of elevation gain.

Route

  • Approximately half-marathon distance (~21.1k).
  • Mostly road, with scenic bits.
  • Strava (obsolete)

Update 2018-4-28: Donut investigation

I did a tour of the donut places I hadn't visited before for donuts.

Stops

  1. The Kilted Donut at Leith market. Strawberry and cream donut. 10/10, would donut again.
  2. The Bearded Baker. Raspberry jam donut. 7/10, solid execution.
  3. The Cuckoo's bakery. Nae donuts! Had a fauxnut, in the form of a cookie sandwich with buttercream. NA/10; good but not a donut.
  4. Lovecrumbs. Nae donuts! NA/10; a zero would look too much like a donut.
  5. Deadly donuts. Nae donuts! Looks dead; a victim of their own deadliness, perhaps.

General observations

  • I ate roughly 1.5 donuts worth (saved the rest) and it was a lot. Including ConsiderIt, Twelve Triangles, and Baba Budan would be a proper challenge.
  • A roughly donut-shaped route may be possible; my abridged route was cruller-shaped.
  • Kilted Donut donuts are really good. Go eat one. Don't use google maps to find them though. It lies.
